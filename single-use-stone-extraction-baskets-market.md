<p><strong>The Single use Stone Extraction Baskets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nitinol Stone Basket</li><li>Stainless Steel Stone Basket</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The single-use stone extraction baskets market includes two primary types: nitinol stone baskets and stainless steel stone baskets. Nitinol stone baskets are made from a nickel-titanium alloy, offering flexibility and shape memory, making them ideal for navigating complex anatomical structures during procedures. In contrast, stainless steel stone baskets provide strength and durability, effectively handling larger stones but may lack the flexibility of nitinol. Both types cater to the needs of urologists in minimally invasive surgeries, promoting convenience and safety by eliminating the risk of cross-contamination.</p></p>
