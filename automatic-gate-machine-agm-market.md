<p><p>Automatic Gate Machines (AGMs) enhance efficiency and security in transit environments. They are categorized into three main types: Arrival Gates control entry for passengers arriving, ensuring smooth flow and access verification. Departure Gates manage the outgoing passenger flow, facilitating ticket validation and crowd management. Two-Way Ticket Checkers efficiently handle both entering and exiting passengers, streamlining operations at busy transit hubs. Together, these systems optimize passenger processing, reduce wait times, and improve overall operational efficiency in transportation.</p></p>
