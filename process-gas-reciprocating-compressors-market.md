<p><p>The process gas reciprocating compressors market is divided into two primary types: single-stage and multi-stage compressors. Single-stage compressors compress gas in one stroke, making them suitable for lower pressure applications. They offer simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, multi-stage compressors compress gas in multiple stages, allowing for higher pressures and increased efficiency in handling larger volumes. This capability makes them ideal for applications in industries such as oil and gas, chemical processing, and power generation.</p></p>
