<p><strong>The Dissolved Oxygen Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical (Luminescent) Type</li><li>Clark Electrochemical (Amperometric) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dissolved oxygen sensors market comprises primarily two types: optical (luminescent) and Clark electrochemical (amperometric). Optical sensors use luminescent technology to measure oxygen levels by detecting the quenching effect of oxygen on a fluorescent light source, offering rapid responses and minimal maintenance. In contrast, Clark electrochemical sensors operate using an electrochemical reaction and require regular calibration, making them reliable for long-term monitoring. Both types are crucial in various applications, including water quality assessment, aquaculture, and industrial processes.</p></p>
