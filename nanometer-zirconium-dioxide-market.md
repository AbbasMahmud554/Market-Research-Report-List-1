<p><p>The nanometer zirconium dioxide market is categorized into three main phases: monoclinic, tetragonal, and cubic. Monoclinic phase zirconium dioxide is known for its stability at lower temperatures and is often used in ceramics. Tetragonal phase is more relevant for high-temperature applications due to its improved mechanical properties and zirconia toughening. Cubic phase zirconium dioxide offers exceptional strength and is widely used in dental applications and advanced materials. Each phase serves unique applications based on its properties.</p></p>
