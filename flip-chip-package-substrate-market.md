<p><strong>The Flip-Chip Package Substrate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FCBGA</li><li>FCCSP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flip-chip package substrate market is primarily segmented into two types: FCBGA (Flip-Chip Ball Grid Array) and FCCSP (Flip-Chip Chip Scale Package). FCBGA utilizes a ball grid array layout, allowing efficient heat dissipation and high-density interconnections, making it suitable for advanced computing applications. In contrast, FCCSP features a chip-scale design that minimizes the package size, enhancing performance for smaller electronic devices. Both types cater to the growing demand for miniaturization and improved performance in consumer electronics and high-performance computing.</p></p>
