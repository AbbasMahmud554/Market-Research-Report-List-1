The IGBT market is categorized into two main types: IGBT modules and discrete IGBTs. IGBT modules are multi-chip packages designed for high-power applications, providing better thermal performance and compactness. Discrete IGBTs, on the other hand, are single devices used in various applications, offering flexibility and ease of integration in lower-power systems.</p></p>
