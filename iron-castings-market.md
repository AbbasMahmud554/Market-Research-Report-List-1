<p><p>The iron castings market encompasses several key types: gray iron, ductile iron, and malleable iron. Gray iron is characterized by its excellent castability and machinability, making it ideal for engine blocks and pipe fittings. Ductile iron, known for its enhanced strength and ductility, is commonly used in automotive and infrastructure applications. Malleable iron, offering good corrosion resistance and toughness, is typically utilized in industrial machine parts and agricultural equipment. Each type serves distinct applications based on its unique properties.</p></p>
