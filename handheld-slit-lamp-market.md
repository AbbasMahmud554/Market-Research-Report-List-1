<p><strong>The Handheld Slit Lamp Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monocular Handheld Slit Lamp</li><li>Binocular Handheld Slit Lamp</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld slit lamp market includes monocular and binocular types. Monocular handheld slit lamps feature a single eyepiece, offering a lightweight, portable option suitable for quick examinations. They are often used in emergency settings or for mobile diagnostics. In contrast, binocular handheld slit lamps provide two eyepieces, enabling enhanced depth perception and comfort during prolonged procedures. This type is favored by clinicians for detailed eye assessments. Both types cater to varying user needs in diagnostic and therapeutic ophthalmology.</p></p>
