<p><p>The Compact Robot Controller market encompasses several types, including Multi-Joint Robot Controllers, Planar Multi-Joint Robot Controllers, and Coordinate Robot Controllers. Multi-Joint Robot Controllers manage robots with multiple articulating joints, allowing for complex movements and flexibility in various tasks. Planar Multi-Joint Robot Controllers operate in a two-dimensional space, making them ideal for tasks requiring horizontal movement. Coordinate Robot Controllers focus on precise positioning along defined axes, ensuring accuracy in straightforward applications, such as material handling or assembly processes.</p></p>
