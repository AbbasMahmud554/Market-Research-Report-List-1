<p><p>The Search and Rescue helmet market comprises several types, including ABS Protective Helmets, HDPE Protective Helmets, and FRP Protective Helmets. HDPE Protective Helmets are known for their durability and resistance to chemicals and impact, ideal for harsh environments. FRP Protective Helmets provide excellent strength-to-weight ratio and thermal resistance, making them suitable for specialized rescue scenarios facing extreme conditions.
