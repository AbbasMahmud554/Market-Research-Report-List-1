<p><p>Changeover switches are essential in power distribution, allowing for seamless switching between two power sources. There are two main types: automatic and manual. </p><p>Automatic changeover switches operate without human intervention, detecting power failures and switching sources instantly to ensure continuous power supply. In contrast, manual changeover switches require human action to switch between sources, providing more control but potentially leading to downtime during the transition. Both types serve critical roles in maintaining power reliability, depending on specific operational needs.</p></p>
