<p><p>The Surgical Anti-Adhesion Products market primarily includes films and gels designed to prevent adhesions following surgical procedures. Films are thin, sterile barriers placed between tissues to minimize the risk of scar tissue formation. They are often made from biocompatible materials that promote healing. Gels, on the other hand, are viscous substances that can be applied directly to tissues, providing a lubricating layer to reduce friction and tissue damage. Both products aim to enhance patient recovery and improve surgical outcomes.</p></p>
