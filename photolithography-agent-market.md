<p><strong>The Photolithography Agent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ArF Immersion</li><li>ArF Dry</li><li>KrF</li><li>I-Line</li><li>G-Line</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photolithography agent market encompasses various types based on the light sources used. ArF immersion uses argon fluoride lasers with a liquid medium to enhance resolution, ideal for advanced nodes. ArF dry employs similar lasers without immersion, suited for high-volume manufacturing. KrF utilizes krypton fluoride lasers, often for smaller features in older technologies. I-Line and G-Line represent older technologies using mercury vapor to produce light at different wavelengths, primarily for less advanced applications. Each type serves distinct roles in semiconductor fabrication.</p></p>
