<p><p>The signal conditioners market is primarily divided into two types: analog signal conditioners and digital signal conditioners. Analog signal conditioners manipulate continuous signals to improve their quality and compatibility with measurement systems, often utilizing amplifiers, filters, and signal converters. In contrast, digital signal conditioners convert analog signals into digital formats for processing, enabling advanced signal analysis and integration with digital systems. Both types are essential for accurate data acquisition, enhancing performance in industrial automation, instrumentation, and various electronic applications.</p></p>
