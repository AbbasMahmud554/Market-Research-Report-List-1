<p><p>The corrugated container board market includes various types based on the number of cardboard layers. A single-layer board consists of one layer of fluted paper sandwiched between two liners, offering basic protection for lightweight items. Two-layer boards add a second layer of fluting, enhancing strength and durability for mid-weight products. Three-layer boards feature an additional layer of corrugation, providing maximum protection and structural integrity for heavy and fragile goods, making them ideal for shipping and storage applications.</p></p>
