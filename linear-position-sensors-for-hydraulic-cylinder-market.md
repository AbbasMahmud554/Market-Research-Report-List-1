<p><p>Linear position sensors for hydraulic cylinders are critical for accurate positioning and control in various applications. Linear Resistance Potentiometers (POTS) offer a cost-effective solution with variable resistance to measure displacement. Linear Variable Inductance Transducers (LVITs) provide high precision and durability, utilizing inductive principles for position sensing. Magnetostrictive Linear Displacement Transducers (MLDTs) deliver high accuracy and are resistant to harsh environments, employing magnetostrictive principles for real-time measurement. Each type caters to specific needs in performance, accuracy, and environmental resistance.</p></p>
