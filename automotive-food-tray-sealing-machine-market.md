<p><p>The automotive food tray sealing machine market is categorized into two main types: semi-automatic and fully automatic food tray sealing machines. In contrast, fully automatic machines streamline the entire sealing process, maximizing efficiency and output with minimal human involvement, ideal for large-scale production. Both types cater to various industry needs, ensuring effective food preservation and packaging.</p></p>
