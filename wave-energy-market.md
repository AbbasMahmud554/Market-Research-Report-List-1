<p><strong>The Wave Energy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oscillating Water Column</li><li>Oscillating Body Converters</li><li>Overtopping Converters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wave energy market comprises three main types: Oscillating Water Columns (OWC), which utilize trapped air in a chamber to drive a turbine; Oscillating Body Converters, where floating devices move with wave motion to generate power; and Overtopping Converters, which capture incoming waves in a reservoir to create hydrostatic pressure that drives turbines. Each type harnesses the kinetic and potential energy of ocean waves, contributing to renewable energy generation and helping to reduce reliance on traditional fossil fuels.</p></p>
