<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Bacterial Endotoxin Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gel Clot Endotoxin Test</li><li>Chromogenic Endotoxin Test</li><li>Turbidimetric Endotoxin Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bacterial Endotoxin Testing market comprises three primary types: the Gel Clot Endotoxin Test, which utilizes a gel formation reaction in the presence of endotoxins; the Chromogenic Endotoxin Test, which employs a colorimetric reaction, making it easy to quantify endotoxin levels; and the Turbidimetric Endotoxin Test, which measures changes in turbidity caused by endotoxin-induced clotting. Each method offers unique advantages in sensitivity, speed, and ease of use, catering to diverse applications in pharmaceuticals and medical devices.</p></p>
