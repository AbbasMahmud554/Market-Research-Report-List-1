<p><strong>The Connected Vehicle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Embedded System</li><li>Tethered System</li><li>Integrated System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Connected Vehicle market comprises three key types: Embedded Systems, Tethered Systems, and Integrated Systems. Embedded Systems are built directly into the vehicle, enabling seamless connectivity without relying on external devices. Tethered Systems connect the vehicle to a smartphone or other personal device, leveraging its capabilities for enhanced functionality. Integrated Systems combine both embedded and tethered features, creating a comprehensive connectivity experience within the vehicle that enhances navigation, entertainment, and communication while ensuring user convenience and safety.</p></p>
