<p><p>Intelligent logistics encompasses two primary market types: cold chain logistics and non-cold chain logistics. Cold chain logistics involves the transportation and storage of temperature-sensitive products, ensuring proper conditions to maintain quality, such as pharmaceuticals and perishable foods. Non-cold chain logistics, on the other hand, deals with goods that are not temperature-sensitive, including durable consumer products and industrial materials. Both markets leverage advanced technologies to enhance efficiency, traceability, and responsiveness, optimizing supply chain operations in their respective domains.</p></p>
