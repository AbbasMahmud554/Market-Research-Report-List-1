<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Induction</li><li>Magnetic Resonance</li><li>Magneto-Dynamic Coupling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless charging system for electric vehicles includes three primary market types: electromagnetic induction, magnetic resonance, and magneto-dynamic coupling. Electromagnetic induction involves transferring energy through inductive coils, suitable for short-range charging. Magnetic resonance operates over larger distances, using resonant coils to enhance efficiency. Magneto-dynamic coupling uses moving magnetic fields for energy transfer, allowing for dynamic charging options. Each method varies in efficiency, distance, and application suitability, shaping the development and adoption of wireless charging solutions for electric vehicles.</p></p>
