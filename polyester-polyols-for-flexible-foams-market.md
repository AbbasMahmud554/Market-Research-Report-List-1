<p><ul><li>Vacuum Melting Method</li><li>Carrier Gas Melting Method</li><li>Azeotropic Vapor Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyester polyols for flexible foams are primarily produced through three methods: Vacuum Melting Method, Carrier Gas Melting Method, and Azeotropic Vapor Method. The Vacuum Melting Method involves melting polyester under reduced pressure to enhance purity and reduce contaminants. The Carrier Gas Melting Method uses an inert gas to facilitate the melting process, improving production efficiency. The Azeotropic Vapor Method relies on solvent removal via azeotropic distillation, ensuring high-quality polyols with controlled molecular weight, suitable for various foam applications.</p></p>
