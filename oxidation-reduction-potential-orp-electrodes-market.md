<p><p>Oxidation Reduction Potential (ORP) electrodes measure the electron activity in solutions, indicating their oxidation or reduction state. The market for ORP electrodes can be divided into two main types: single junction and double junction. Single junction electrodes consist of one internal reference solution, providing simplicity and lower cost but are more prone to contamination. Double junction electrodes feature two reference solutions, enhancing accuracy and stability by minimizing interference and contamination, making them ideal for complex or harsh chemical environments.</p></p>
