<p><ul><li>Rotary Friction Welder</li><li>Linear Friction Welder</li><li>Friction Stir Welder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The friction welder market consists of three main types: Rotary Friction Welders, which use rotational motion to generate heat and bond materials; Linear Friction Welders, which utilize linear oscillation for welding, ideal for specific applications like connecting rods; and Friction Stir Welders, which employ a rotating tool to mechanically mix materials at the joint, creating a solid-state weld. Each type caters to different industrial needs, offering unique advantages in terms of efficiency, strength, and material compatibility.</p></p>
