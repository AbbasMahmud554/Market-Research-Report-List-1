<p><p>The online invoicing software market can be categorized into cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based invoicing software is hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access it via the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and automatic updates. It typically operates on a subscription model. In contrast, on-premises software is installed locally on a user's hardware, providing complete control over data but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. Both types cater to different business needs and preferences.</p></p>
