<p><p>The Surface Management Temperature Sensor market is categorized into two main types: contact-type and non-contact-type temperature sensors. Contact-type sensors measure temperature through direct contact with a surface, providing accurate readings for solid or fluid surfaces. In contrast, non-contact sensors assess temperature from a distance using infrared technology, making them ideal for moving objects or hazardous environments. Together, these sensors enhance temperature monitoring and management across various industries, ensuring safety and efficiency in applications like manufacturing, HVAC, and food processing.</p></p>
