<p><p>Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCoS) Spatial Light Modulators (SLMs) are devices that manipulate light for various applications, including display technology and optical processing. The market is segmented into two types: reflective and transmissive LCoS-SLMs. Reflective LCoS-SLMs use a reflective surface to control light, making them suitable for applications requiring high brightness, such as projectors. Transmissive LCoS-SLMs, on the other hand, allow light to pass through, commonly used in applications like goggles or augmented reality systems, emphasizing versatility in optical performance.</p></p>
