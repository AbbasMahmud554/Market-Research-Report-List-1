<p><p>The Cast Resin Dry-Type Transformers market is segmented into AN (Naturally-Cooled) and AF (Forced-Air-Cooled) types. AN transformers utilize ambient air for cooling, relying on natural convection, making them suitable for environments with minimal cooling requirements. In contrast, AF transformers employ forced air ventilation to enhance cooling efficiency, catering to installations with higher power demands and reduced space. Both types provide robust electrical performance, safety, and reduced environmental impact, making them ideal for industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
