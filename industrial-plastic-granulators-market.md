<p><p>The industrial plastic granulators market encompasses various types designed for specific applications. Beside-the-press granulators are compact machines that operate alongside injection molding machines for immediate recycling. Central granulators are larger units suitable for high-volume processing in centralized systems, efficient for various materials. Heavy-duty granulators are built for tough applications, handling robust plastics with ease. Thermoforming granulators are specialized for recycling scrap from thermoforming processes, ensuring efficient material recovery and minimizing waste across the production line.</p></p>
