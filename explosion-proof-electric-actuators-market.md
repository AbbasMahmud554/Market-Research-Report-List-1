Linear actuators, on the other hand, convert electrical energy into linear motion, ideal for pushing or pulling applications. Both types are designed to operate safely in explosive atmospheres, adhering to stringent safety standards, ensuring reliable performance in industries like oil and gas, chemical processing, and mining.</p></p>
