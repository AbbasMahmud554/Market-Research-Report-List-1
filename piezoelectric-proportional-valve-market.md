<p><strong>The Piezoelectric Proportional Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Valves with Bender Element</li><li>Jet Valves with Multilayer Actuator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piezoelectric proportional valve market encompasses two main types: valves with bender elements and jet valves with multilayer actuators. Valves with bender elements utilize piezoelectric materials to convert electrical signals into mechanical movement, allowing precise control of fluid flow. Jet valves with multilayer actuators incorporate multiple layers of piezoelectric material, enhancing responsiveness and control capabilities, making them suitable for high-speed applications. Both types offer advantages in precision, energy efficiency, and compact design, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
