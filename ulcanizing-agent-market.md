<p><p>The vulcanizing agent market is categorized primarily into two types: hardeners and accelerators. Hardeners, such as sulfur and peroxides, facilitate the cross-linking process in rubber, enhancing its durability and heat resistance. Accelerators, on the other hand, speed up the vulcanization process, reducing production time while improving efficiency. Both types play crucial roles in the rubber industry, with hardeners ensuring optimal performance characteristics and accelerators enabling faster manufacturing processes, ultimately enhancing product quality and consistency.</p></p>
