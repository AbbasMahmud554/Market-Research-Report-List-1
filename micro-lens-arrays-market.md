<p><p>The micro-lens arrays market comprises two primary types: aspherical micro-lens arrays and spherical micro-lens arrays. Aspherical micro-lens arrays feature lenses with a non-spherical shape, optimizing light collection and reducing aberrations, making them ideal for applications in imaging and laser systems. In contrast, spherical micro-lens arrays utilize traditional spherical shapes, often providing simpler manufacturing processes and cost-effective solutions for various optical applications. Both types play essential roles in enhancing optical performance across sectors like telecommunications, consumer electronics, and medical devices.</p></p>
