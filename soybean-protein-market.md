<p><p>The soybean protein market primarily consists of three types: soybean protein isolates, concentrates, and hydrolysates. Soybean protein isolates have the highest protein content, typically over 90%, making them ideal for food applications requiring high protein. Soybean protein concentrates contain around 70% protein and are often used in meat alternatives and snacks. Soybean protein hydrolysates are pre-digested proteins offering improved solubility and digestibility, commonly utilized in dietary supplements and functional foods. Each type serves distinct needs in the food and nutrition industries.</p></p>
