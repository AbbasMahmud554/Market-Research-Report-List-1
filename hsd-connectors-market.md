<p><p>HSD Connectors encompass three main market types: Wire to Wire (W2W), Wire to Board (W2B), and Board to Board (B2B). Wire to Wire connectors facilitate direct connections between two cables, offering flexibility in various applications. Wire to Board connectors link wires to circuit boards, ensuring secure electrical connections in devices. Board to Board connectors interconnect multiple circuit boards, crucial for complex electronic systems, promoting efficient signal transmission and space-saving designs in modern technology.</p></p>
