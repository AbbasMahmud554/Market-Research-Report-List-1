<p><p>An Ethernet switch is a networking device that connects multiple devices within a local area network (LAN), allowing them to communicate and share resources efficiently. It operates at the data link layer (Layer 2) of the OSI model, directing data packets to their intended destinations based on MAC addresses. A router, on the other hand, is a device that connects different networks and routes data between them, functioning at the network layer (Layer 3). Routers manage traffic between external networks, such as the internet and local devices, enabling data packet forwarding and route selection.</p><p>The Ethernet switch and router market is anticipated to experience substantial growth, driven by the increasing demand for high-speed internet and the expansion of cloud-based services. The adoption of IoT devices and the need for enhanced network security are further propelling market evolution.
