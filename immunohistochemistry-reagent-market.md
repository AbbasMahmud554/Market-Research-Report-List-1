<p><p>The immunohistochemistry reagent market is segmented into several types. Histological stains are used to enhance tissue visualization and differentiate cellular components. Blocking sera and reagents are employed to reduce background noise and improve specificity during antibody binding. Chromogenic substrates generate a colored product upon reaction with enzymes, aiding in the detection of antigens. Other reagents may include secondary antibodies, buffers, and polymer-based detection systems, which collectively enhance the sensitivity and accuracy of immunohistochemical assays.</p></p>
