<p><strong>The Multi-layer Flexible Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Top Loading</li><li>Bottom Loading</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Multi-layer Flexible Packaging market can be categorized into top loading and bottom loading types based on the packaging design and functionality. Top loading packaging allows products to be inserted from the top, facilitating easy access and efficient filling for items like bags or cartons. In contrast, bottom loading packaging enables products to be inserted from the bottom, providing stability and space optimization, which is ideal for heavy or bulk items. Both types enhance product protection and shelf life while offering convenience for consumers.</p></p>
