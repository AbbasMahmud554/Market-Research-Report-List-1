<p><p>Transcranial Magnetic Stimulators (TMS) are non-invasive devices used for brain stimulation to treat various neurological and psychiatric conditions. The market includes several types: </p><p>- sTMS (single-pulse TMS) delivers isolated pulses to targeted brain regions.</p><p>- pTMS (paired-pulse TMS) assesses connectivity between brain areas using paired pulses.</p><p>- rTMS (repetitive TMS) employs repeated pulses to induce long-lasting changes in neuronal activity, often used for depression.</p><p>- nTMS (navigated TMS) integrates imaging technology for precise targeting during therapy or research.</p></p>
