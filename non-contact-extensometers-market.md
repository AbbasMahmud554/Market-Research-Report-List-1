<p><p>The non-contact extensometer market primarily includes laser and video extensometers, which are used for precise strain measurements in materials during testing. Laser extensometers utilize laser beams to measure displacement without physical contact, ensuring high accuracy and preventing sample interference. Video extensometers employ high-resolution cameras to capture images of the specimen, analyzing video footage to determine strain by tracking surface features. Both technologies facilitate efficient and reliable assessment of material properties, enhancing research and quality control processes in various industries.</p></p>
