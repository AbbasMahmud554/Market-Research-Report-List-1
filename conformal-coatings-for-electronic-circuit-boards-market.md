<p><p>Conformal coatings for electronic circuit boards serve as protective layers that enhance reliability and longevity. The market is primarily divided into water-based and solvent-based types. Water-based coatings are environmentally friendly, easy to apply, and offer excellent moisture resistance, making them suitable for various applications. In contrast, solvent-based coatings provide superior adhesion and durability but may emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Both types are critical in safeguarding electronic components from environmental factors such as humidity, dust, and chemicals.</p></p>
