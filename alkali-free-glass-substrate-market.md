<p><p>The alkali-free glass substrate market is primarily divided into two types: alkali-free aluminosilicate glass and alkali-free borosilicate glass. Alkali-free aluminosilicate glass offers enhanced thermal stability and mechanical strength, making it ideal for applications in electronics and high-temperature environments. On the other hand, alkali-free borosilicate glass is renowned for its excellent chemical resistance and low thermal expansion, often used in laboratory glassware, optical devices, and various industrial applications. Both types cater to specific technological needs and industry requirements.</p></p>
