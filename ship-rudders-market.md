<p><p>The ship rudders market comprises several types, including spade, semi-balanced, and flap rudders. Spade rudders are characterized by a flat blade, providing efficient steering with minimal drag. Semi-balanced rudders feature a pivot point that enhances maneuverability while reducing steering effort. Flap rudders include movable flaps that increase lift and control during navigation, offering superior responsiveness. Each type caters to different ship designs and operational needs, allowing for optimized performance and improved handling in various maritime conditions.</p></p>
