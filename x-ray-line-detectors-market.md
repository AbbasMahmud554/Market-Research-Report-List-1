<p><p>X-ray line detectors are essential in imaging applications, classified into three main types. Single-energy detectors capture images using a single X-ray energy level, offering basic material differentiation. Dual-energy detectors utilize two distinct energy levels to enhance contrast and material characterization, improving diagnostic accuracy. Multi-energy detectors further advanced this technology by acquiring multiple energy levels simultaneously, enabling detailed analysis of complex materials and tissues. Each type serves specific needs in medical imaging, security screening, and industrial inspection.</p></p>
