<p><ul><li>Conventional Ligation</li><li>Self-Ligating Brackets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aesthetic orthodontic bracket market comprises two main types: conventional ligation brackets and self-ligating brackets. Conventional ligation brackets require elastic ligatures to secure the archwire, offering aesthetic options like clear or tooth-colored materials. In contrast, self-ligating brackets utilize a built-in mechanism to hold the wire, reducing friction and often leading to faster treatment times. Both types aim to improve patient comfort and aesthetic appeal while facilitating effective tooth alignment and orthodontic treatment outcomes.</p></p>
