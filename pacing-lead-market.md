<p><strong>The Pacing Lead Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unipolar Pacing Lead</li><li>Bipolar Pacing Lead</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pacing lead market types refer to unipolar and bipolar pacing leads used in cardiac pacing devices. Unipolar pacing leads have a single electrode positioned at the tip, with a return electrode typically located within the device itself, offering a simpler design and lower cost. In contrast, bipolar pacing leads feature two closely spaced electrodes that enhance electrogram quality and reduce stimulation threshold, leading to improved patient outcomes. Both types cater to different clinical needs and preferences within the cardiology sector.</p></p>
