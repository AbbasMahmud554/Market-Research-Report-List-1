<p><p>The multi-material packaging market includes various polymer types known for their unique properties. Polyethylene (PE) is flexible and moisture-resistant, making it ideal for bags and films. Polypropylene (PP) offers excellent tensile strength and is widely used in containers. Polyamide (PA) provides high barrier properties and is often found in food packaging. Ethylene Vinyl Alcohol (EVOH) is recognized for its superior oxygen barrier, enhancing product shelf life. Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) is popular for bottles and trays, valued for its clarity and recyclability.</p></p>
