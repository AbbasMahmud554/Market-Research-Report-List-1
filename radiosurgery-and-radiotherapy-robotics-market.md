<p><p>Radiosurgery and radiotherapy robotics markets comprise various treatment modalities, each utilizing specific radiation types. X-ray based systems, such as linear accelerators, deliver targeted high-energy photon beams to precisely target tumors. Gamma-ray based treatments leverage radioactive sources, typically employing devices like GammaKnife to focus beams on lesions. Proton-beam based therapies utilize charged particles, offering a unique advantage by depositing the majority of their energy directly at the tumor site, minimizing damage to surrounding healthy tissues. Each type is integral for tailored cancer treatment.</p></p>
