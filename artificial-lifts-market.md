<p><strong>The Artificial Lifts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pump Assisted</li><li>Gas Assisted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Artificial lift systems are essential for enhancing oil and gas production from wells. The market can be segmented into two primary types: pump-assisted and gas-assisted methods. Pump-assisted lifts utilize mechanical pumps to elevate fluids from the reservoir, enhancing flow rates, especially in low-pressure conditions. In contrast, gas-assisted lifts employ injected gas to lower fluid density, enabling easier flow to the surface. Both types are crucial for optimizing extraction processes and increasing overall production efficiency in the industry.</p></p>
