<p><strong>The Sillcon Steel Sheet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Orientation</li><li>Non-oriented</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicon steel sheet market can be categorized into two main types: oriented and non-oriented. Oriented silicon steel sheets are designed to have high magnetic permeability in specific directions, making them ideal for applications like transformers and electric motors, where efficient magnetic performance is crucial. Non-oriented silicon steel sheets, on the other hand, offer uniform magnetic properties in all directions, making them suitable for components like rotating machinery and electrical appliances, where omnidirectional magnetic performance is needed.</p></p>
