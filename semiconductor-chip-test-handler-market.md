<p><strong>The Semiconductor Chip Test Handler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity Handlers</li><li>Turret Handlers</li><li>Pick-and-Place Handlers</li><li>Strip Handlers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor chip test handler market includes four main types: </p><p>1. **Gravity Handlers** rely on gravity to transfer chips between positions, offering simplicity and efficiency.</p><p>2. **Turret Handlers** utilize a rotating system to manage multiple testing sites, optimizing throughput.</p><p>3. **Pick-and-Place Handlers** use robotic arms to precisely pick and place chips, enhancing accuracy and flexibility for diverse chip sizes.</p><p>4. **Strip Handlers** handle strips of chips simultaneously, streamlining the testing process for mass production. Each type serves specific testing needs in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
