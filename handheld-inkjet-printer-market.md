<p><p>The handheld inkjet printer market can be categorized into two primary types: USB and photoelectric interface printers. USB handheld inkjet printers connect directly to devices, enabling easy data transfer and immediate printing capabilities, making them popular for on-demand labeling and marking. On the other hand, photoelectric interface printers utilize sensors to detect products and automate the printing process, enhancing efficiency in production environments. Both types cater to diverse applications, from industrial use to consumer labeling, driving market growth.</p></p>
